How to Play

Minesweeper is a classic digital game where you have to uncover all the spaces that do not contain a mine. If you select a game with a mine, then the game ends and you lose.

When you click on a space that doesn't hold a mine, the space will reveal how many mines that particular space is next to. Can you clear all the spaces without touching a mine?
